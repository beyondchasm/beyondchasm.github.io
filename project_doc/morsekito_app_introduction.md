# MorseKito (모스키토) 🦟
> **"Modern Morse, Modern Retro"**

MorseKito is a premium, pixel-art inspired Morse code toolkit designed for both enthusiasts and practical users. With its unique "Mosquito-like" sensory feedback system and clean modern aesthetics, MorseKito makes the ancient language of Morse code feel fresh and accessible.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Dual-Engine Translation (텍스트 & 모스 부호 변환) 🔁
- **Text to Morse**: Instantly convert complex text into precise Morse signals.
- **Morse to Text**: Decode incoming light or sound signals back into readable language.
- **Support**: Optimized for both **English (Latin characters)** and **Korean (Hangeul)**.

### 2. Multi-Sensory Playback (입체적 신호 재생) 🔊⚡️🔦
- **Buzz (Vibration)**: Experience the unique "Morse-Buzz" rhythm on your handheld device.
- **Flash (Light)**: Communicate across distance using the device's built-in flashlight.
- **Sound (Beep)**: Classic acoustic signals with modern clarity.

### 3. Smart History & Private Library (기록 및 저장소) 📁
- **Auto History**: Never lose a translation with the automatic local history log.
- **Saved Signals**: Curate your own library of frequently used signals (SOS, Greetings, etc.).
- **Quick Access**: Rename and load saved signals directly into the translation engine with one tap.

### 4. Interactive Reference Chart (모스 부호표) 📖
- Browsable list of all alphabetical and numerical Morse codes.
- Tap any symbol to hear and feel its rhythm instantly—perfect for learning.
